    Excellence

    When actively used and studied, emotional intelligence opens up what excellence is to one self, family, school, or organization. Excellence is the practise of being the best version of yourself! At Takafa Mindset, our objective is to bring out excellence within!

    Trust

    Emotional intelligence allows you to be open and honest about your being and feelings. When you can understand this, you can trust yourself to make the best decisions that will better serve yourself and community. This is a core value we hold true at Takafa Mindset, in order for one to move into being more emotionally intelligent, we form a place of trust to be open and honest with each other and ourselves.

    Empathy

    One of the cornerstones of emotional intelligence is empathy. Empathy is the practise of understanding and share how someone is feeling. Not only do we practise and teach how to empathize with others, we first focus on how we can empathize with ourselves. It is when we can empathize with ourselves, we can truly be empathetic with the community around us.

    Commitment

    We are committed to everyone we serve and work with. We show this by working with our clients to understand what their goals are, so that at the end of our engagement, we can reach your organizations desired outcome!

    Innovation

    We are constantly training, learning and better ourselves so we can better you! Partnering with world leaders like Six Seconds to equip our team with the best tools, assessments and research to support your organization!

    Who We Are

    Takafa Mindset is a social organization which focuses on building the socio-emotional skills of people so they can become the change they want to see in their communities.

    We noticed a need for socio-emotional skills in organizations of all kinds and we believe that socio-emotional skills are important in fostering inclusive prosperity. These skills are fundamental and indispensable in building fair, just, healthy, and prosperous communities. Therefore, through our organization we aim to provide this element which has been missing in the personal development of individuals, the positive development of organizations, and the sustainable development of communities.

    OUR MISSION

    We seek to empower minds and magnify human strengths for sustainable social and economic change as well as positive development. Our goal is to do this through the usage of cutting-edge neuroscience combined with mindfulness practices. This will help individuals, schools and organizations increase their performance and competitiveness.

    OUR VISION

    Be the main catalyst of a profound and positive transformation of people, organizations, and communities.

    Volunteer

    Through our project “Resonant Africa” we work to build vibrant schools around Africa by introducing emotional intelligence to schools which are willing to experiment it. For that purpose and since we cannot be everywhere at once, we need volunteers across the continent to help us achieve that goal. Volunteers will serve as local coordinators of the Resonant Africa Project in their communities. If you are interested in joining the Resonant Africa Team, kindly reach out to us to learn more about the opportunity.
    Likewise, in order to serve as many Africans as possible our work requires a lot of bilingual (English-French) translation and you may volunteer to help us with that. Should you be interested in helping us with translation, kindly reach out.

    Donations

    Our “Resonant Africa” project is a non-profit project with the aim to build vibrant schools in Africa through emotional intelligence. Our goal is to reach as many schools and students as possible. Therefore we need your help to achieve that goal; send us your donation today. We will be grateful to have you chip in building a new generation of empathetic, compassionate, and high-performing African citizens.

    Funding Support

    We seek funding to sustain the “Resonant Africa” project so as to build more vibrant schools across the continent. If you can provide us with funding or help us have access to any funding support please contact us. We will be grateful to have you chip in building a new generation of empathetic, compassionate, and high-performing African citizens.
